Data Deletion Request

BanglaBracket — Brings World Cup 2026 · Last updated: 30 June 2026

You can ask us to delete your BanglaBracket account and all personal data associated with it at any time. This includes your phone number, name, email, any Bkash number, any location signal, your bracket predictions, and — for verified users — your encrypted identity details.

How to request deletion

Send a message from the email or phone number on your account to:

Email: data.delete@banglabracket.com

with the subject line "Delete my account", and include the name, phone number, or email you used to sign up so we can locate your account.

What happens next

  1. We confirm the request comes from the account owner.
  2. We permanently delete your account and associated personal data from our database, normally within 30 days.
  3. We email you to confirm the deletion is complete.

Note: if you signed in using Google or Facebook, deleting your BanglaBracket data does not delete your Google or Facebook account — it only removes the data we hold. You can also remove BanglaBracket's access from your Google or Facebook account security settings at any time.

A note on prizes

If you request deletion while a prize claim is in progress, deleting your data will end that claim, as we can no longer verify eligibility. We may retain the minimum information required by law for completed prize payouts.
