Privacy Policy

BanglaBracket — Brings World Cup 2026 · Last updated: 26 June 2026

BanglaBracket ("we", "us") is a free online game where players predict the results of the 2026 FIFA World Cup for a chance to win a prize. This policy explains what we collect, why, how we protect it, and how you can have it deleted. We collect only what the game needs and nothing more.

Who runs this service

BanglaBracket is operated as an independent project. For any privacy request or question, contact us at support@banglabracket.com.

Public display

Your display name and profile photo may appear publicly on the leaderboard and Winners Wall, and may be shared by BanglaBracket on social media if you rank highly or win. See our Terms for details.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.
  • We don't store ID images in our database. If you email your ID to verify, it stays in that email only, and we delete it when and if you ask.
  • We do not show ads inside the game or share your data with advertisers.
  • We do not track your precise GPS location or follow you across other websites.

How we protect your data

  • All traffic is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Sensitive identity details (ID number, date of birth) are encrypted at rest using AES-256.
  • Login sessions use secure, http-only cookies; we never store your password (sign-in is passwordless or via Google/Facebook).
  • Access to administrative data is limited to the operator and recorded in an audit log.

Sign-in with Google and Facebook

If you choose these options, we receive a unique account identifier and, where permitted, your name and email, solely to create and secure your account. We request only basic profile and email permissions. We do not post anything to your social accounts. Your use of Google and Facebook is also governed by their own privacy policies.

How long we keep it

We keep your account and prediction data until you ask us to delete it. When you request deletion, we permanently delete all of your data within 30 days (see below). Where you have been paid a prize, we may retain the minimum information required by law.

Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To do so, see our Data Deletion page or email data.delete@banglabracket.com.

Children

BanglaBracket is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be posted on this page with a new date.
